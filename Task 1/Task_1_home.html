About me

Sam "Shadow Seeker" Kraft, a prodigious linguist and cryptographer, graduated top of his class from MIT with degrees in Astrophysics and Computer Science, attracting the attention of the covert Directorate of Extraterrestrial Surveillance (DES). As the lead secret agent for DES, Sam excels in gathering intelligence on alien activities and technologies. Fluent in over ten Earth languages and capable of decoding alien dialects, Sam is also an expert hacker skilled in advanced cyber-espionage, stealth operations, and combat. Notable for intercepting an alien transmission to prevent an invasion, infiltrating an alien spacecraft to retrieve critical technology, and forming covert alliances with friendly extraterrestrial species, Sam is driven by a deep sense of duty and secrecy, committed to protecting humanity.
